Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

Crypto staking is the process of actively participating in transaction validation on a proof-of-stake (PoS) blockchain. By staking your coins, you help secure the network and in return earn rewards. It's similar to earning interest in a traditional savings account, but typically with much higher returns.

Yes, security is our top priority. We use industry-leading security measures including:

  • Multi-signature wallets
  • Cold storage for the majority of funds
  • Regular third-party security audits
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) for all accounts
  • Insurance coverage for digital assets

Rewards are calculated based on several factors:

  • The staking plan you choose (Starter, Advanced, or Pro)
  • The amount of crypto you stake
  • The duration of your stake
  • Whether you choose compounding or simple rewards

Our platform automatically calculates and distributes rewards according to the terms of your staking plan. You can track your rewards in real-time through your dashboard.

Each staking plan has a specific lock-up period during which your funds cannot be withdrawn. However, we do offer an early unstaking option with the following conditions:

  • A 10% penalty fee on the staked amount
  • Forfeiture of all pending rewards
  • Processing time of 3-5 business days

We recommend carefully considering your investment timeline before staking to avoid early withdrawal penalties.
